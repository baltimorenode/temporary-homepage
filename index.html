You can help contain COVID-19!

In response to the growing need for personal protective equipment (PPE) for healthcare workers during this time of crisis, We the Builders has launched a collaborative project to manufacture face shields for local hospitals. Go to http://mdmakersunite.com/ now to join the effort.

If you don't have the equipment to help make face shields, there's still lots you can do! Check out OSI Baltimore's list of COVID-19 resources, including multiple "Community-Based Resources" that you can contribute to right away.
